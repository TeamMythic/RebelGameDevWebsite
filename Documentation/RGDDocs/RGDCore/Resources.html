Resources

- Unity provides a great way to get assets without making a field. To make your own folder you can make it under Assets/Resources/Members --> your gitName.

Example On How To Instantiate A Prefab From Resources

                               
    var obj = Resources.Load("RGDStarters/FirstPersonController", typeof(Transform)) as Transform;     
    Instantiate(obj, transform.position, transform.rotation);
