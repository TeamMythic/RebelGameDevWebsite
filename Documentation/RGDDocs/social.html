Social

- A big part of the Open Source Project is the social aspect of the club.

Projects

The main focus of the open source project is the JRPG. Even though the big community game is the JRPG you can still make any games in your personal or team folders. For example, you can make custom games, mini games, assets, scenes, music, even components to help other people. The sky is your limit!

Social Folder

You are allowed to create 1 personal folder with your name(you can create team folders as well, if given a team name). We still are in the beta for the Open Source Project, so there is not a whole lot going with fully built sections. Once we get enough games/worlds developed by people, we will post the worlds section where you can post your world for everyone to see. For now people can still see your worlds if you tell them your folder name and where in the folder to access the scene. For personal folder use your github name so we know who is tied to what folder when pushing.

Future of Social

The Open Source is great, but the officers and I are working on something that we think you will find quite extraordinary regarding avatars and the social aspect of the game. Please be patient as our team is working none stop to provide you with the best game development frame-work at UNLV.
