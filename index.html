Community

Discover the World of Game Development

At our club, we believe in empowering aspiring game developers to break out of their comfort zones and start creating extraordinary games. We hold dynamic in-person meetings every Thursday at 4:30 PM, where students come together to connect, share ideas, and collaborate on exciting projects. Whether you're a programmer, artist, musician, game tester, storyboarder, or simply passionate about games, there's a place for you here.

Our community thrives on diversity, welcoming game developers of all skill levels and backgrounds. We offer a supportive environment where you can make friends, build valuable industry connections, and grow your skills through hands-on experience. If you're eager to dive into the world of game development or expand your expertise, we invite you to join our Discord server and start chatting with our vibrant community today. Together, we'll turn your creative visions into reality.
